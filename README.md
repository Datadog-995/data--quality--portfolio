# Cleaned Butcher Sales Portfolio

## Business Problem
The butcher shop faces challenges with data inconsistency in its sales records, such as non-standardized meat cut names and anomalies in weights, which impact accurate revenue and inventory tracking.

## Action/Reasoning Table
| Action | Reasoning |
| --- | --- |
| Meat Cut Standardization | Ensures uniform categorization of products across all transactional entries to prevent misclassification and improve sales analytics accuracy. |
| Weight Anomaly Flagging | Detects outliers and entry errors in weights to maintain inventory integrity and prevent revenue loss from inaccurate measurements. |

## Tools Used
- Python Pandas
- OpenRefine
- Google Sheets

## Business Insights
Standardizing the data reveals true product sales velocities and flags anomalous weight recordings, allowing the business to recover lost revenue margins and optimize procurement strategies.
